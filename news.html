New Workshop

We are very excited to announce a new series of unique and intimate workshops in 2020. All of our workshops are built around small groups, so as to foster proximity to the project in a comfortable and inviting environment, and also assures you dedicated time and attention from the teacher. The teachers are all experienced artists themselves and are among some of the best in their field.

All are held in English and therefor open for international participants.
